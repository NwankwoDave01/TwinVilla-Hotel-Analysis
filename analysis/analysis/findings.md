## 1. Spending Trend (Weekly)
- Week 1 had the highest overall spending due to large maintenance purchases.
- Week 2 showed reduced spending but a higher number of transactions.
- Week 3 and 4 maintained medium spending with more consistent departmental activity.

## 2. Top Spending Department
Maintenance consistently ranked as the top department in most weeks.
This indicates:
- frequent repairs,
- replenishment of materials,
- and operational dependency on maintenance.

## 3. Categories Driving Costs
- Transport
- Generator fuel
- Electrical repairs
- Cleaning materials

## 4. Cost Leakage Indicators
- Multiple small, repeated purchases of the same items
- High transport frequency
- Repeated emergency repairs

## 5. Operational Suggestions
- Bulk purchase frequently used items
- Weekly maintenance budget allocation
- Assign fixed suppliers for stable pricing
- Introduce approval workflow for repeated purchases
